Computer Intelligence Project

About

Computer Intelligence Project is a non-profit and open scientific collaboration focused on incubating open-source projects and research. Our mission is similar to BigCode Project. We conduct cutting-edge research on multimodal code intelligence, build data pipelines, develop the open corpus, train large models, and evaluate the models.
